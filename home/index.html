FAQ

01. How do I start playing?

You need at least 11 Ephereals (NFTs) to start playing when the game launches. You can get an entire team in our Marketplace or you can hire other people's Ephereals using EPH.

02. What is EPH?

It's the utility token required to perform several in-game actions such as hiring and training players, competing in leagues and tournaments, playing against other teams in PvP matches, and improving the infrastructure of your team. It's also the token you will earn for winning matches, completing challenges or using our marketplace —among many others.

03. What is an NFT?

A Non Fungible Token can be used to represent ownership of unique digital items. Each token has individual attributes. This makes it possible to tokenize game items and enable real world value economies. Game items can be freely bought, sold and traded on NFT marketplaces outside of the game or peer-to-peer in exchange for cryptocurrencies.
