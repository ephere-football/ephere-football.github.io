Ephereals

Ephereals are at the core of our game: your football players.

Technically they are NFTs with mental and physical attributes, trainable football skills and distinctive visual properties. Their lives improve when used in-game: they gain experience, they get tired, can get injured, and they also grow old.

Ephereals are alive!

By owning them, you'll be able to:

  • Use them in your own team
  • Sell or trade them
  • Earn passive income by allowing other people to use them in their own teams

Play to earn

Ephere is a football management simulation game. It revolves around managing your own football team which includes hiring Ephereals, setting the team's lineup, ideating the in-match strategy, and improving the infrastructure (training facilities, stadium, etc.)

Players can earn EPH —Ephere's Token— in several ways, including:

  • Completing game challenges
  • Finishing at the top of the league rank
  • Lending Ephereals to other players
  • Selling Ephereals
  • Winning P2P matches
  • Winning seasonal tournaments
  • (and many more still in the works)
